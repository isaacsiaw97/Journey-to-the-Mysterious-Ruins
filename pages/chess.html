The Chess Gardens

As the adventurers enter the gardens, the take stock of what's around them. In the gardens, there are strawberries, cabbages, carrots, tomatoes, and grapes growing. As the adventurers look around further, they see that the checkerboard pattern on the ground is seperated into 3 8x8 grids. Suddenly, ambush! As the adventurers step closer towards the statues, they spring to life and attack our heroes! Thinking quickly, one of the adventurers uses something from the garden to disract and defeat most of the statues while the rest of the adventurers fought them.
